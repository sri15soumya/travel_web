Historic Monuments

India Gate

India Gate is a war memorial dedicated to soldiers who died in World War I. This iconic structure stands as a symbol of patriotism and resilience. The evening lights and surrounding gardens make it a popular spot for tourists and locals alike. The Amar Jawan Jyoti, an eternal flame, burns here in tribute to the fallen soldiers.

Cost: Free

Timings: Open 24 hours

Qutub Minar

The Qutub Minar, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is the tallest brick minaret in the world. Built in the 12th century, it is an architectural masterpiece adorned with intricate carvings and inscriptions from the Quran. The surrounding Qutub Complex also features several historic structures, making it a fascinating place to explore.
