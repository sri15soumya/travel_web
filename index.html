Delhi

The capital of India, Delhi is a vibrant mix of history and modernity. It is home to iconic landmarks like the Red Fort, Qutub Minar, India Gate, and the Lotus Temple. The city is a hub for politics, business, and culture, with bustling markets and delicious street food.

book now

Jaipur

The "Pink City" of Rajasthan, Jaipur is known for its grand palaces, forts, and vibrant markets. Key attractions include Hawa Mahal, Amber Fort, City Palace, and Jantar Mantar. The city is famous for its Rajasthani cuisine, jewelry, and textiles.

book now

Shimla

The capital of Himachal Pradesh, Shimla is a picturesque hill station known for its colonial-era buildings, The Mall Road, Ridge, and the scenic toy train. It's a popular retreat for summer vacations and winter snowfall.

book now

Kolkata

Known as the "City of Joy," Kolkata is the cultural heart of India. It is famous for its colonial architecture, Howrah Bridge, Victoria Memorial, and rich literary heritage. The city is also known for Durga Puja celebrations and its love for sweets like rasgulla and sandesh.

book now

Goa

A coastal paradise, Goa is famous for its stunning beaches, Portuguese heritage, vibrant nightlife, and delicious seafood. Tourists flock here for water sports, beach parties, and the laid-back lifestyle.

Book Now

Mysore

A city in Karnataka known for its royal heritage, Mysore boasts the magnificent Mysore Palace, Chamundi Hill, and the famous Dasara festival. It is also known for silk sarees, sandalwood products, and as a center for yoga.

book now
